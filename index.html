ABOUT US

DANCE YOUR HEART OUT was recently founded in 2023 by Hannah Trisha Mercado. The co-founders have seen how SoulCycle made spinning a new popular form of exercise, and the studio has now become a lifestyle brand. They envisioned to make dance the next big sporting and fitness category. DANCE YOUR HEART OUT focuses on various urban dance styles such as house, popping, hiphop, whacking, breaking, dancehall, trump, and heels.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide professional dance instruction and instill an appreciation for the art of dance in a safe, high-quality studio environment. We promote strong self-confidence, self-respect, discipline, and appreciation through the fine arts of dance. We inspire every dancer to find the creativity inside theirselves as "we dance to tel a story" each year.

VISION

We envision DANCE YOUR HEART OUT to provide support for the community with uplifting activities, opprotunities for joyful present moment engagement, providing excellence in educational, cultural, and recreational experiences. We will provide opportunities for connecting with art, nature, music, dance and otehr positive activities, while celerating creativity and diversity in a supportive joyful community.
